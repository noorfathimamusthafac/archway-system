Archway Design Standards 2024: Workspace Zoning
Zoning and Circulation:
- Main corridors: Minimum 1500mm width for high traffic zones (Reception to Cafeteria).
- Secondary aisles: Minimum 1200mm between workstation clusters.
- Cafe/Dining Seating: Minimum 450mm between chair back and wall; 900mm for main service aisles.
- Fire Exit Paths: Must differ by region but generally >1200mm clear width.

Acoustic Zoning:
- EXECUTIVE ZONES must be acoustically separated from BREAKOUT ZONES.
- Use baffled ceilings (NRC > 0.8) in open office areas.
- Meeting rooms require STC 45+ partitions.

Lighting & Ambiance Guidelines
Lux Levels (Target):
- Workstations: 350-500 Lux at desk height.
- Corridors: 150 Lux.
- Meeting Rooms: 500 Lux (dimmable).

Color Temperature:
- Work areas: 4000K (Cool White).
- Breakout/Relaxation: 3000K (Warm White).

Wet Area Standards & Waterproofing 2024
Toilets & Pantry:
- Waterproofing Standard: 2-coat Crystallization-based Cementitious Pressure Decomposition (CPD) coating.
- Vertical Upturn: Minimum 150mm high vertical upturn on all partition walls.
- Pond Testing: Mandatory 48-hour pond testing (minimum 50mm water level) before tiling.
- Pipe Sleeves: PVC sleeves with non-shrink grout (GP2 type) at all slab penetrations.
- Slope: Minimum 1:80 slope towards floor drains.
- Drains: 100mm dia PVC pipes with cockroach traps.
